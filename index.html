Skills

What I can help with.

Cinematographer

I craft visuals that speak. Through light, composition, and movement, I turn ideas into cinematic frames that carry mood, depth, and intention. Every shot is deliberate-built to tell a story, not just capture one.

Sound Engineer

I shape sound that feels real. From clean recordings to immersive mixes, I build audio that adds weight and emotion to every project. Precision, clarity, and atmosphere drive everything I create, using tools like Pro Tools.

Video Editor

I cut with purpose. I take raw footage and turn it into sharp, engaging stories through pacing, rhythm, and visual flow. Clean transitions, strong color, and tight edits—delivered using Adobe Premiere Pro and DaVinci Resolve.

Front-End Developer

I build digital experiences that work and look right. Clean code, responsive layouts, and smooth interactions-focused on clarity and performance. I turn ideas into functional interfaces using Next.js.

About

My story, my craft, and the vision behind the work.

Heywood Lenwii David is a multidisciplinary creative from Ka-Lorre Town in Khana Local Government Area, Ogoni, Rivers State, Nigeria. Born on the 10th of October, 2003. his journey reflects a strong passion for media production, visual storytelling, and digital creativity. He has worked with several media production companies, including Joonwyyt Entertainment and BCS Starcross Television, gaining top-notch experience in event coverage, movie production, sound engineering, music videos, video and sound editing, graphics design, and frontend development. His work blends creativity, technical skill, and a clear commitment to producing professional results across both visual and digital platforms.
